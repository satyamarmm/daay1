Making Tea

1.Boil water in a pot.

2.Add tea leaves and sugar.

3.Let it simmer for a few minutes

4.Strain the tea into a cup and add milk if desired.

Making Paratha and Aloo Curry

1.Boil tha aloo firstly.

2.Roll out the dough and cook on a hot griddle

3.For allo curry , saute onions, tomatoes and spices

4.Add boiles potatoes and cook untill the curry thicknes

Making Biryani

1.Take the spices

2.Cool basmati rice untill 70% done.

3.Meat/vegetables and rice in a pot.
